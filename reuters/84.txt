Texas Instruments Inc said it hasdeveloped the first 32-bit computer chip designed specificallyfor artificial intelligence applications.    The company said the new microprocessor, measuringone-centimeter square and containing 553,687 transistors, isthe densest chip ever made.    The chip was designed, Texas Instruments said, for use withthe Lisp langugage, which is used widely by softwareprogrammers in the artificial intelligence field.    The company added that it is working on a productionversion of the chip. Reuter&#3;
