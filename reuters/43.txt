Sugar imports subject to the U.S.sugar import quota during the week ended January 9, the initialweek of the 1987 sugar quota year, totaled 5,988 short tonsversus 46,254 tons the previous week, the AgricultureDepartment said.    The sugar import quota for the 1987 quota year(January-December) has been set at 1,001,430 short tonscompared with 1,850,000 tons in the 1986 quota year, which wasextended three months to December 31.    The department said the Customs Service has reported thatweekly and cumulative imports are reported on an actual weightbasis and when final polarizations are received, cumulativeimport data are adjusted accordingly.
