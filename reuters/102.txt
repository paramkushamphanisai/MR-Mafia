Ford Motor Co said its Ford MotorCredit Co on April One will redeem 4.0 mln dlrs of its 8.70 pctdebentures due April 1, 1999.    It said the debentures are redeemable at a price of 100 pctof the principal. Because April 1, 1987 is an interest paymentdate on the debentures, no accrued interest will be payable onthe redemption date as part of the redemption proceeds.    Debentures will be selected for redemption on a pro ratabasis, Ford said. Reuter&#3;
