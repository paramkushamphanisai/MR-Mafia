Schult Homes Corp announced an initialpublic offering of 833,334 units at five dlrs per unit, saidJanney Montgomery Scott Inc and Woolcott and Co, managingunderwriters of the offering.    They said each unit consists of one common share and onewarrant to buy one-half share of common.    The warrant will entitle holders to buy one-half commonshare at 5.50 dlrs per full share from March one, 1988, toSeptember one, 1989, and thereafter at 6.50 dlrs per full shareuntil March 1991, they said.
