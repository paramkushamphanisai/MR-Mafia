The Commodity Credit Corporation, CCC,has accepted an export bonus offer to cover the sale of 37,000long tons of wheat flour to North Yemen, the U.S. AgricultureDepartment said.    The wheat four is for shipment March-May and the bonusawarded was 119.05 dlrs per tonnes and will be paid in the formof commodities from the CCC inventory.    The bonus was awarded to the Pillsbury Company.    The wheat flour purchases complete the Export EnhancementProgram initiative announced in April, 1986, it said. Reuter&#3;
