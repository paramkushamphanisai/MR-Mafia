The eight major New York City banks had64 mln dlrs in average borrowings from the Federal Reserve inthe week to Wednesday February 25, a Fed spokesman said.    The week marked the second half of the two-week bankstatement period that ended on Wednesday. The banks had noborrowings in the prior week.    Commenting on the latest week, a Fed spokesman said thatall of the borrowing occurred yesterday and was done by fewerthan half of the banks.
